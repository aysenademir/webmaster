Our Story

Building a stronger community together

How We Started

Founded in 2023, the Community Resource Hub began as a grassroots initiative to connect local residents with essential services and support.

What started as a simple neighborhood directory grew from volunteer efforts and local meetups. We partnered with organizations, added verified listings, and expanded into the hub you see today.

Our Mission

We believe every person deserves easy access to the services they need. Our mission is to connect people to local programs, help organizations share their offerings, and empower communities with accurate information.

We prioritize accessibility, inclusivity, and collaboration. We build tools and run outreach that reduce friction for people seeking help and keep partners connected and informed.

Our Impact

Since our inception, we have:

  • Connected over 5,000 residents with essential services
  • Partnered with 100+ local organizations
  • Facilitated 1,000+ successful resource connections
  • Organized 50+ community outreach events

We measure impact through feedback and partnership reports. These insights inform where we focus outreach and how we improve services over time.
