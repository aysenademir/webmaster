Our Mission

We connect people to local services and support networks so everyone can find help when they need it. Our mission focuses on accessibility, collaboration, and measurable impact across neighborhoods.

Browse Resources

Accessibility First

We design tools and guides so people can find services quickly — online and in-person. Multiple contact options and plain-language information are core to our work.

Partner Collaboration

We work with nonprofits, health providers, and local government to keep listings accurate and connect programs efficiently to residents.

Measure Impact

Our team tracks outcomes and feedback so we can prioritize outreach and improve the services most needed by the community.

How we work

We verify listings, highlight trusted partners, and publish clear step-by-step guidance to help people act quickly. Our volunteer network supports outreach and helps collect on-the-ground feedback.

We prioritize equity in our outreach, ensuring services are reachable by people with different needs — multilingual materials, accessible meeting locations, and digital-friendly forms.
